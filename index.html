Kathleen Maiorana is an event producer with sixteen years of event planning exeperience in varied industries. Kathleen provides superior client communication and project leadership. Her extensive knowledge in project management, budgeting, logistics, creative development and conference management has made her an invaluable resource to her clients.

Kathleen's clients include: Leukemia and Lymphoma Society, Public Interst Projects Autism Speaks, University of Maryland Children's Hospital, Cool Kids Campaign, Kidsbridge Museum, Trinity College and Ronald McDonald House Charities of Baltimore.

Before forming KBM Event Consulting LLC, Kathleen was the Events Manager at Proskauer, a prominent New York law firm. There she oversaw more than 200 annual sponsorships, dinners, seminars and retreats. Prior to that, Kathleen was an Event Producer at Baltimore's premier event production company, Feats, Inc., managing complex, multi-faceted productions across the country for a variety of corporate and non-profit organizations. Kathleen began her career in New York's fast-paced financial industry, at Deutsche Bank, where she gained global experience running conferences, seminars and training retreats for a variety of audiences.

Kathleen earned her Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from Towson University.
